Our Policies

We want you to feel confident and informed from the start. The following policies are intended to give you a general sense of what to expect when working with us. Please note that the specific terms and conditions for your event will be outlined in your individual service agreement and proposal, which serve as the binding terms of record.

Booking & Reservations

All events require a signed service agreement and a non-refundable deposit to secure your date. Deposit amounts vary depending on the scope of services and will be outlined in your custom proposal. We recommend booking as early as possible, as dates fill quickly — especially during peak seasons.

Payment Terms

A deposit is required at the time of booking to reserve your date, with the remaining balance due in advance of your event. Specific deposit amounts, payment deadlines, and accepted payment methods will be outlined in your service agreement. Please note that certain payment methods may be subject to processing fees.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances change. If you need to reschedule or cancel, please contact us as soon as possible. Cancellation terms — including any applicable forfeiture of deposit or partial charges — are based on how far in advance notice is provided and will be specified in your service agreement. Generally, earlier notice allows for greater flexibility.

Guest Count & Menu Changes

Final guest counts and menu selections are due in advance of your event, with the specific deadline outlined in your service agreement. We will do our best to accommodate increases after the deadline, though additional fees may apply. Please note that reductions to the guest count after final confirmation may not result in a change to the quoted price.

Travel & Delivery

Events within our standard service area are included in base pricing. Events beyond this range may be subject to a travel fee, which will be noted in your proposal. Setup and breakdown logistics will be coordinated with your venue and detailed in the service agreement.

Liability & Insurance

Gather Catering and Events carries liability insurance for all events we service. Clients are responsible for obtaining any permits required by their venue or local regulations, including alcohol service permits when applicable. We are happy to assist with the permitting process.

Food Safety & Allergies

Our team follows all applicable health and food safety regulations. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies when planning your menu. While we take every precaution to accommodate dietary needs, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment and are not responsible for allergic reactions.
