Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services, pricing, and what to expect when you work with Gather.

Pricing

What does Day-Of Coordinating cost?

Our Day-Of Coordinating package starts at $2,500. This includes pre-event planning sessions, vendor confirmations, a detailed timeline, and full on-site coordination from setup through the final farewell.

What does Full Planning cost?

Full Planning starts at $2,950. This is our most comprehensive package: we're with you from the very first vendor call through the last dance, handling every detail along the way.

Do you offer partial planning?

Yes. Partial Planning starts at $1,250 and is ideal for couples who have already made headway on planning but want a professional to step in and manage the remaining details and day-of logistics.

How is catering staffing priced?

Catering staffing is billed at an hourly rate of $100–$125 per staff member. Final pricing depends on the scope of your event and the number of hours needed. We'll walk you through an accurate estimate during your consultation.

Do you require a deposit to hold my date?

Yes. A deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your date. Specific amounts and payment terms are outlined in your service agreement. We recommend booking early, as our calendar fills up quickly, especially during peak seasons.

Services

What types of events do you coordinate?

We coordinate a wide range of events including wedding ceremonies, receptions, rehearsal dinners, and engagement parties. We also work with corporate clients, nonprofits, and private celebrations of all sizes.

What types of weddings do you work with?

We work with all wedding styles and traditions, including civil, cultural, destination, elopement, eco-friendly, military, non-religious, and religious ceremonies. Every couple is unique, and we tailor our approach to your vision.

What planning services does Gather offer?

We offer three levels of planning support: Day-Of Coordinating (for couples who've done the planning and just need expert execution), Partial Planning (for couples who want help finishing what they've started), and Full Planning (for couples who want a dedicated partner from the very beginning).

Do you provide bartending and catering separately from planning?

Yes. Our Mobile Bartending, Catering, and Catering Staffing services can be booked independently or bundled with planning and coordination. We're happy to put together a custom package that fits your needs and budget.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend reaching out at least 6–12 months before your event, especially for weddings and larger productions. That said, we always encourage you to contact us even if your date is sooner; we'll let you know what's available.

Do you provide bar service at dry venues?

Yes, with some conditions. Our Mobile Bartending service can operate at dry venues depending on the venue's policies and any applicable local permits. We're happy to serve non-alcoholic and specialty mocktail menus at venues that don't allow alcohol. For venues that do permit alcohol service, we can help guide you through the permitting process. Reach out and we'll walk through the details together.

Can services be bundled or packaged?

Absolutely. Our services are designed to work together, and many clients combine planning or coordination with catering, bartending, or staffing. We're happy to build a custom package that fits your event and budget. Booking multiple services with one team also means tighter communication and a more seamless experience on your event day. Request a quote and we'll put something together for you.

What is included in catering staffing?

Our Catering Staffing service covers the behind-the-scenes work that keeps your event running smoothly, including coordinating with your caterer on event details, setting up the buffet, replenishing food throughout service, bussing tables as needed, tearing down at the end, and assisting with additional cleanup. Staffing is billed at $100–$125 per hour per staff member with a 4-hour minimum.

Logistics

Do you travel outside of Cincinnati or Columbus?

Yes. We serve events within a 100-mile radius of our base location at no additional travel charge. Events beyond that range may be subject to a travel fee, which will be outlined in your custom proposal. If you're unsure whether your venue falls within our service area, reach out and we'll let you know.
