Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when submitting an inquiry through our website. This may include your name, email address, phone number, event date, estimated guest count, event type, services of interest, budget, and any additional details you share in the comments field. We do not collect information through any other means on this website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare proposals, and communicate with you about our catering and event services. When you submit an inquiry, you will receive an automated confirmation email, and our team will receive a notification with your details so we can follow up. We do not use your information for any purpose unrelated to providing our services.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Your information is only accessible to authorized members of the Gather Catering and Events team for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and providing our services.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, including responding to inquiries and providing services. If you would like your information removed from our records, please contact us and we will promptly comply with your request.

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any other tracking technologies. We do not collect any information about your browsing behavior, and no data is shared with advertising or analytics platforms.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites, such as The Knot and WeddingWire. These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit through links on our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request corrections to inaccurate information, and to request deletion of your information. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below, and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at info@gathercateringandevents.com.
